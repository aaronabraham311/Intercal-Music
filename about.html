Our Story

In the summer of 2016, Kevin and Lawrence held a small Father's Day performance at Dana Village, a local senior home, in order to share their passions and talents with the community. Today, they wish to provide the same unique opportunities to other students around the city.

Music is a medium that unites. InterCal wishes to use the power of music as a means to connect youth musicians, as well as allowing them to interact with seniors in our community. We aim to create everlasting moments for not only all of our musicians, but also our audience members.
